## Summary

## Key Research Findings

### Fora Travel
- Modern "Shopify for travel agents" — $49/mo or $299/yr, 70-80% commission split
- $1B+ bookings, 10K+ advisors, $78.5M funding
- **No GDS access** — focused on hotels, not flights
- Not the right fit for Ronnie's interest (flights)

### Host Agencies with GDS (for flight bookings)
| Agency | Monthly Fee | Commission Split |
|--------|-----------|-----------------|
| Outside Agents | ~$20-30/mo | 70/30 → 90/10 |
| Nexion Travel Group | ~$20-30/mo | 70/30 |
| KHM Travel Group | ~$40-50/mo | 70/30+ |
| Travel Planners International | ~$40-50/mo | 70/30 |

### Flight Commission Reality
- US domestic: **0%** commission
- International: **1-9%**, Asian carriers most generous (5-9%)
- Asiana specifically: ~5-7% on intl tickets, merging into Korean Air late 2026

### Consolidator Route (Better Option)
- **Centrav** and **GTT Global** offer net fares 10-20% below published
- Agent marks up and keeps the difference
- No GDS skills needed (Centrav is point-and-click)
- Better margins than commission: potentially $400-1,000+ per premium cabin ticket
- Need host agency credentials (IATA/TRUE) to register with consolidators
